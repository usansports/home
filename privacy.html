Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-08-22

Our Commitment

NeonBreaker is designed to be a fun, accessible game that respects your privacy. We do not require account creation and are committed to minimal data collection.

Information We Handle

  • Game Data: High scores and preferences are stored locally in your browser’s `localStorage`. This data is never sent to our servers.
  • Contact Form: If you contact us via the form on our homepage, we collect the name and email address you provide solely to respond to your inquiry.

Cookies and Analytics

NeonBreaker does not use tracking cookies. We may use privacy-respecting, anonymous analytics to count page visits, but we do not track individual users across sessions.

Your Control

  • You can clear your locally stored high scores by clearing your browser's site data for NeonBreaker.
  • Using your browser's private or incognito mode will prevent any data from being stored locally.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please use the contact form on the Home page.
