Frequently Asked Questions

Your common questions about NeonBreaker, answered.

Is NeonBreaker mobile-friendly?

Absolutely. The game is fully responsive and optimized for both touch and keyboard controls, ensuring a great experience on desktops, tablets, and smartphones.

How is my progress saved?

Your high scores and game settings are saved directly in your browser's localStorage. This means your progress is kept on your device without needing an account.

Can I reset the leaderboard?

Yes. To clear your local leaderboard data, open your browser's developer console and run the command: localStorage.removeItem('neonbreaker_leaderboard'). Refresh the page to see the change.

What controls are supported?

Use the Left and Right arrow keys on your keyboard to move the paddle. On mobile, simply touch and drag the paddle.

What technology does NeonBreaker use?

The game is built with pure, optimized vanilla JavaScript and the HTML5 Canvas API for maximum performance. We do not use any heavy game engines or frameworks.
