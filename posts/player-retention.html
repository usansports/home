Optimizing Session Length

Design play sessions that respect time while encouraging repeat visits.

Published 2025-08-22

Short, satisfying rounds keep players engaged without feeling locked in, making it simple to pause and exciting to return.

Natural Breaks

Create clear stopping points—like level ends or checkpoint saves—so players can step away without losing progress.

Save on Exit

Automatically store high scores and current state so a quick close never costs the player their achievements.

Return Prompts

Show last best scores or unfinished challenges on the start screen to spark another run the next time they launch.
