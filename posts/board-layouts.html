Designing Fair Difficulty Curves

Keep players engaged with challenges that feel tough yet achievable.

Published 2025-08-22

Smart pacing ensures excitement without frustration, letting players feel in control as the game intensifies.

Start Generous

Give newcomers early wins and clear successes to build momentum and confidence before the real test begins.

Increase Gradually

Raise speed, enemy count, or complexity in small steps so players adapt naturally while staying alert.

Signal the Threats

Use visual cues or audio hints so dangers are predictable and skill—not luck—drives survival.
